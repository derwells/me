## Document Identity

- **Title:** Revenue Memorandum Order No. 31-2019
- **Date:** June 18, 2019
- **Subject:** Revised policies and guidelines for establishing and revising the schedule of zonal values of real properties
- **Legal basis:** Section 6(E) of the NIRC of 1997 as amended by Section 4 of RA 10963 (TRAIN Law)
- **Supersedes:** RMO No. 41-2010 and OPM-AS-APMD 2017-02-06
- **Related circular:** RMC 06-2021 (clarifications on compliance with RMO 31-2019)

## Committee Structure

### Three-Tier Committee System

| Committee | Level | Chair | Members |
|-----------|-------|-------|---------|
| **STCRPV** (Sub-Technical Committee on Real Property Valuation) | RDO level | Revenue District Officer (RDO) | ARDO (Vice-Chair), Municipal/Assistant City Assessor, Local Development Officer (Office of the Mayor), 2 licensed appraisers from reputable association |
| **TCRPV** (Technical Committee on Real Property Valuation) | Regional level | Regional Director | Reviews/validates STCRPV submissions per Annex E guidelines |
| **ECRPV** (Executive Committee on Real Property Valuation) | National/DOF level | — | BLGF is a member; conducts final review |

### Approval Chain
```
STCRPV (prepares schedule)
  → public posting (30 days) + newspaper (3 consecutive weeks)
    → TCRPV (reviews per Annex E)
      → ECRPV (final review)
        → DOF Secretary signs Department Order
          → publication in Official Gazette or general circulation newspaper
            → effective 15 days after publication
```

## Key Provisions

### Valuation Methodology
- STCRPV considers three recommended values: BIR, private appraisers, provincial/city/municipal assessor
- Final recommended value = **average of the two (2) highest recommended values**
- Based on "accepted appraisal methods, most recent sales records, and other data"

### Revision Frequency
- Mandatory automatic adjustment every **3 years** (per TRAIN Law amendment to NIRC Section 6(E))
- In practice, 38% of schedules are outdated (per DOF 2024 data)

### Tax Base Rule (pre-RPVARA)
- Zonal value applies provided it is **higher than** both:
  1. FMV per provincial/city assessor schedule
  2. Gross selling price per notarized deed
- i.e., tax base = max(SP, ZV, Assessor FMV)

### Publication Requirements
- Public posting in municipality for 30 calendar days
- Weekly publication in local newspaper for 3 consecutive weeks
- If no objection received → forwarded to TCRPV
- Approved schedule published in Official Gazette or newspaper of general circulation
- Effective 15 days after publication

### Absence of Members
- If private appraisers absent or fail to attend: Chair proceeds with average of two highest recommended values or best data available
- Must submit to TCRPV: invitation letters with waiver, Affidavit, Maps, Minutes of Meeting, all related documents

## Annex C — Schedule of Recommended Zonal Values (Standard Format)

### Header Structure (per-barangay block)

```
BUREAU OF INTERNAL REVENUE
SCHEDULE OF [APPROVED/RECOMMENDED] ZONAL VALUES OF REAL PROPERTIES
REVENUE REGION NO. [X] - [REGION NAME]
REVENUE DISTRICT OFFICE NO. [XX] - [RDO NAME]

Province:           [PROVINCE]
City/Municipality:  [CITY/MUNICIPALITY]     D.O. NO.          [DO NUMBER]
Zone/Barangay:      [BARANGAY NAME]         Effectivity Date   [DATE]
```

### Standard Column Layout (4 columns)

| Column | Position | Header Text (varies) | Content |
|--------|----------|---------------------|---------|
| Street/Subdivision | Col 0 | `STREET/SUBDIVISION`, `STREET NAME / SUBDIVISION / CONDOMINIUM`, `STREET/SUBDIVISION/TOWNHOUSES/CONDOMINIUMS` | Street name, subdivision name, or condo building name |
| Vicinity | Col 1 | `VICINITY`, `V I C I N I T Y` | Boundary descriptors, landmarks, cross-streets |
| Classification | Col 2 | `CLASSIFICATION`, `CLASSI-FICATION`, `CLASSIFI-CATION` | Code from Annex B |
| Zonal Value | Col 3 | `ZV/SQ.M.`, `ZV / SQM`, `[Nth] REV. ZV/SQM`, `INITIAL ZV/SQ.M.` | Philippine Pesos per square meter |

### Column Header Variations Observed in 31 Workbooks

**Column 0 (Street):**
- `STREET/SUBDIVISION` (most common in early revisions)
- `STREET NAME / SUBDIVISION / CONDOMINIUM` (Pangasinan 2023)
- `STREET/SUBDIVISION/TOWNHOUSES/CONDOMINIUMS` (Makati 2021)

**Column 2 (Classification):**
- `CLASSIFICATION` (ideal)
- `CLASSI-FICATION` (hyphenated due to narrow column, very common)
- `CLASSIFI-CATION` (alternative hyphenation)

**Column 3 (ZV):**
- `INITIAL ZV/SQ.M.` (first revision only)
- `[Nth] REVISION ZV/SQ.M.` or `[Nth] REV. ZV / SQM` (subsequent revisions)
- The revision number is embedded in the column header, not a separate column

## RMC 06-2021 — Compliance Clarifications

**Source:** Web search extraction (PDF at blgf.gov.ph returned 403)

### Key Content
- Reiterates the requirement for RDOs to comply with RMO 31-2019 format
- **BLGF compliance concern:** The BLGF (as ECRPV member) noted that Schedules of Zonal Values presented at the executive level were **not compliant** with the RMO
- Issues that should have been resolved by STCRPV/TCRPV were being escalated unnecessarily
- Enjoins assessors to actively participate in preparation and review of recommended zonal values
- Directs assessors with ongoing revisions to inform BLGF of significant unresolved issues

## Embedded Fallback Guidelines (from workbooks)

The workbooks themselves contain implementation guidelines (observed in DO 51-87 for East Makati, likely standardized via Annex E):

### Fallback Rule 1: No Previous Sale of Particular Classification
> Where no zonal value has been prescribed for a particular classification of real property, the zonal value prescribed for the **same class of real property located in the adjacent barangay of similar conditions** shall be used.

### Fallback Rule 2: No Previous Sale of Any Classification
> In a barangay where no sale, exchange or other disposition of land has been effected, the approved zonal value of a **similarly situated property in an adjacent barangay of similar conditions** shall be used.

### Fallback Rule 3: No Previous Sale on Particular Street
> Where a street in any barangay does not appear in the approved list, the zonal value of the **nearest street of similar conditions** within the same barangay shall be used.
